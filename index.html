About Us

Our Aerospace Team consists of passionate students dedicated to exploring the frontiers of aerospace engineering. Founded in 2023, we've grown from a small group of enthusiasts to a competitive team representing our school at national competitions.

We specialize in designing and building UAVs, rockets, and innovative aerospace solutions. Our diverse team brings together skills in engineering, programming, physics, and design to create cutting-edge projects.

Through teamwork, dedication, and creative problem-solving, we strive to push the boundaries of what student aerospace teams can achieve.

Our Work

Here's a showcase of our teams work!

Airbrakes

Our implementation of airbrakes, helping us reach target altitudes with high accuracy during competitions.

CircuitSpace

An custom-built circuit planner for rocketry, designed to teams easily plan and visualize complex avionics setups.

Rocket++

Our custom C++ library developed to make configuring and controlling our airbrakes and other rocket systems easy and efficient.

Built Rocket

A photo of our completed competition rocket, representing countless hours of design, building, and testing by our dedicated team members.

Our Teams

Three specialized teams driving aerospace innovation

⛽

Liquids Team

Developing small-scale liquid rocket engines using gaseous oxygen and hydrocarbon fuels for independent research and static fire testing.

🚀

ARC Team

Competing in America's largest rocketry challenge with 5,000+ students nationwide. Top teams launch near D.C. and present at Capitol Hill.

🌌

High Power Rocketry

Building advanced rockets with 160+ newton-seconds impulse, featuring dynamic control surfaces, avionics, and scientific payloads.
